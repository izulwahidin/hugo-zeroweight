The Jorie Center has integrated the latest advancements in pharmacotherapy by offering highly effective, **FDA-approved weight loss injections** as part of its personalized, medically managed protocol. These injectable medications belong to a class known as Glucagon-like Peptide-1 Receptor Agonists (GLP-1 RAs). They function by mimicking the naturally occurring GLP-1 hormone released in the gut after eating. This hormone then targets specific areas of the brain that regulate appetite and satiety. By doing so, the medications lead to a significant **reduction in appetite and food intake**, and they also slow gastric emptying, helping the patient feel fuller for longer. This two-pronged action facilitates substantial and sustainable weight loss when combined with the Jorie Center's established diet and counseling program. The integration of these powerful tools ensures clients are leveraging the **highest standard of medical practice** available in weight management.
