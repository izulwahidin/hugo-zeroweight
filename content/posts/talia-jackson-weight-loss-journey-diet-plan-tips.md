### HIIT and Endurance: The Power of Varied Cardiovascular Activity

Jackson’s regimen strategically combined high-intensity interval training (HIIT) for maximum immediate fat burning with endurance-building activities like steady-state running and cycling. The inclusion of HIIT is particularly effective because these short, explosive bursts of effort followed by brief recovery periods significantly increase the body's calorie burn both during and long after the workout is complete—a phenomenon known as the **"afterburn effect"** (Excess Post-Exercise Oxygen Consumption or EPOC).

By alternating HIIT with longer, moderate-intensity endurance sessions, she trained her heart and lungs while improving stamina and functional fitness, ensuring the process remained both challenging and sustainable. This strategic variation is crucial for preventing plateaus and keeping the body constantly adapting to new stimuli.

### Building Lean Muscle: The Importance of Strength and Weight Training

Talia understood that building lean muscle mass is non-negotiable for long-term weight maintenance. By increasing her muscle mass, Jackson effectively boosted her **Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)**, ensuring she burned more calories 24/7. This fundamental shift—where strength training accelerates fat loss by improving metabolism—is a core principle for any successful and sustainable body transformation blueprint.
