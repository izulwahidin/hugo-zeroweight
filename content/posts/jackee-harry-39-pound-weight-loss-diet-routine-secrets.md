### The Power of Rest: Why Naps and Sleep Are Her 'Secret Weapon'

Jacké Harry has consistently highlighted the profound importance of rest, naming water and regular naps as crucial elements of her routine. This focus on rest underscores a key principle of sustainable weight management: adequate sleep directly impacts energy levels and, more importantly, metabolic function. The body manages energy, stress, and appetite through a delicate hormonal balance, and chronic sleep loss severely disrupts this balance.

Research from institutions like the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) confirms this by showing a clear link between short sleep duration and an increased risk of obesity. Specifically, insufficient sleep causes metabolic and endocrine alterations, including a decrease in the satiety hormone leptin and an increase in the hunger-stimulating hormone ghrelin. This hormonal shift creates an elevated drive to eat, often overriding conscious efforts at diet control. Therefore, prioritizing a full eight hours of sleep and strategically incorporating "power naps" can act as a powerful tool to naturally regulate appetite, mitigate the stress-related emotional eating that leads to weight gain, and support the body’s natural energy regulation systems.
