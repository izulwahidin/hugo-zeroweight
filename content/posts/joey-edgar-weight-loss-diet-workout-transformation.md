This is precisely why it is favored in rapid, dramatic celebrity body transformations—it allows for maximal results from minimal time investment, provided the intensity is genuine. Edgar's inclusion of resistance training alongside HIIT ensured his weight loss was primarily fat, not muscle, resulting in a toned, healthy physique.
