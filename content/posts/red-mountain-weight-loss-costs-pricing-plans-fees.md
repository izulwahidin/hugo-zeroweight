### Q2. Can I Use My HSA/FSA Funds to Pay for the Program?

Yes, in many cases, you can use funds from a Health Savings Account (HSA) or Flexible Spending Account (FSA) for your medically supervised weight loss program and prescription medications. The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) generally considers these expenses eligible if they are prescribed to treat a specific disease diagnosed by a physician, such as obesity, hypertension, or diabetes.

To ensure your funds qualify, you may need a **Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN)** from the licensed medical provider that clearly states your diagnosis and explains why the weight loss program or medication is required as part of your treatment plan. The strong focus on medical supervision and the use of prescription medication in the Red Mountain programs aligns well with the criteria for being a qualified medical expense, but you should always confirm with your specific HSA/FSA plan administrator before making a purchase.
