## The Duchess’s Daily Fuel: Deconstructing the Flexible Plant-Based Diet

### Vegan During the Week: Maximizing Fiber and Nutrients

The Duchess of Sussex follows a dietary pattern she describes as "Flexitarian." This strategy is centered on consuming a primarily plant-based diet throughout the workweek, reserving meat and other animal products for occasional enjoyment. This means her meals from Monday through Friday are packed with legumes, whole grains, fruits, and vegetables, aiming for maximal fiber and nutrient intake. This focus on whole, unprocessed plant foods naturally reduces the consumption of saturated fats and cholesterol while simultaneously increasing critical micronutrients and beneficial compounds. The discipline of adhering to a mostly plant-based structure during the week provides her with a solid foundation of healthy eating without the psychological burden of a permanent, restrictive diet.

### Weekend Flexibility: The 'Everything in Moderation' Mindset

The key to the longevity and sustainability of her approach lies in the "flex" part of Flexitarianism. On weekends, she allows for the inclusion of meat, fish, and other indulgences. This principle of conscious, moderate inclusion is strongly affirmed by nutritional experts. Registered Dietitian Dawn Jackson Blatner, who popularized the Flexitarian diet, emphasizes that the approach allows individuals to reap the benefits of vegetarian eating—such as weight management and reduced risk of chronic diseases—while still enjoying animal products occasionally. A Registered Dietitian's (RD) analysis highlights that this flexibility can actually aid in the nutritional adequacy of the diet, as the occasional inclusion of animal products, particularly lean meats or fish, can help supply highly bioavailable sources of essential nutrients like Vitamin B12, Iron, and Zinc, which can sometimes be lower in strictly vegan diets. This balanced incorporation ensures she maintains a healthy and varied nutrient profile while preventing feelings of deprivation that often sabotage more rigid dieting attempts.

### The Power of Green Juice: Her Secret to an Afternoon Energy Boost

While many people reach for a second cup of coffee for a midday pick-me-up, the Duchess relies on a "food-as-medicine" philosophy, using a custom green juice blend as her favorite afternoon energizer. This isn’t a small shot but a full, custom blend, often cited to contain a mixture of spinach, kale, apple, and lemon.
