Instead of counting calories, which can be tedious and confusing, she learned to view food through the lens of a daily points budget.

We can establish the **authenticity and official nature** of this method by referencing her well-documented tenure as a WW spokesperson. Jennifer Hudson publicly partnered with Weight Watchers from **2010 to 2014**, a period during which she achieved her major transformation. This official collaboration lends significant **credibility** to the claim that the program's structure—specifically its focus on education and flexibility—was the primary engine of her success. The system taught her to prioritize nutrient-dense, lower-point foods and to view higher-point indulgences as an occasional treat, not a daily staple.
