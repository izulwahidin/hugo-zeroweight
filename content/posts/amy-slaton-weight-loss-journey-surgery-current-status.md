This strategy is crucial because the smaller stomach pouch requires high-quality nutrition to maximize nutrient absorption and promote satiety (a feeling of fullness) while minimizing the intake of calorically dense, nutrient-poor foods like simple sugars and high-starch items.

As she navigated her new reality, Amy often shared her meal tactics. She revealed in interviews and on the show that her meals became simplified and protein-focused.
