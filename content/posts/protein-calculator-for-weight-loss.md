### Comparing Protein: Whey, Casein, Soy, and Plant-Based Options

When focusing on muscle retention during a dieting phase, you need to concentrate on **leucine-rich sources**. * **Whey Protein:** This is the gold standard for rapid delivery and is exceptionally high in leucine, making it ideal for post-workout recovery or a quick, high-quality serving.
* **Casein Protein:** Derived from milk, like whey, casein digests much slower, providing a sustained release of amino acids. This makes it an excellent option for overnight use or for maintaining satiety between meals.

* **Soy and Other Plant-Based Options:** While plant proteins can meet EAA needs, they often have a lower leucine content or overall protein percentage per serving.
