### Understanding Mounjaro (Tirzepatide): Mechanism and Purpose

The medication Lambert chose to use is **Mounjaro (tirzepatide)**, a prescription drug primarily indicated for type 2 diabetes but also widely used for chronic weight management. Lambert has openly shared that he used this **GLP-1 receptor agonist** to help regulate his blood sugar levels, and, crucially, to significantly reduce his appetite and eliminate intense food cravings. This category of medication is scientifically proven to enhance the body's natural processes related to hunger and satiety. Mounjaro works by mimicking two natural hormones: Glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) and Glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide (GIP). This dual action means the medication can increase the production of insulin when blood sugar is high and, perhaps most relevant for weight loss, slow down gastric emptying and directly signal to the brain that the stomach is full. By making the body feel fuller for longer, it fundamentally makes it easier to maintain the necessary **caloric deficit** essential for weight loss.
