### Actionable Step 2: Implement High-Intensity Circuit Training (HICT)

Janet Jackson's trainer used a High-Intensity Circuit Training (HICT) model to achieve the singer’s results. This approach is characterized by performing multiple resistance exercises consecutively with minimal rest between sets, keeping the heart rate elevated throughout the session. This is the mechanism that allowed Jackson to achieve such a powerful transformation while keeping her workouts to an efficient 45-60 minutes.

A sample HICT circuit based on her principles would involve 3-5 non-stop weight exercises performed back-to-back. The non-negotiable rule is to minimize the rest period—aim for 15-30 seconds max between exercises and only 60-90 seconds between full circuits. This strategy is highly effective because it simultaneously taxes both the muscular and cardiovascular systems.
