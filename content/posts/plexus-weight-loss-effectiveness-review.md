### Exercise and Hydration: Essential Complements to the Plexus Journey

Physical activity and proper hydration are non-negotiable elements of successful weight management, acting as essential complements to the Plexus products. Exercise not only increases caloric expenditure but also improves insulin sensitivity and boosts mood.

Furthermore, adequate water intake is absolutely crucial, particularly when consuming high-fiber products like Plexus Slim Hunger Control. Ingredients such as Polydextrose need water to move efficiently through the digestive system and maximize their swelling/satiety benefits. Insufficient water intake when consuming high levels of dietary fiber is the most common cause of digestive discomfort, such as constipation. Aim to drink at least eight glasses (64 ounces) of water daily, increasing this amount with every serving of a high-fiber supplement, to ensure comfort and maximize the feeling of fullness and support digestive function.
