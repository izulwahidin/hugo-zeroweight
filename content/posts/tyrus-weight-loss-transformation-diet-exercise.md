According to a meta-analysis published in the *Journal of Sports Medicine*, **low-impact aerobic exercise, particularly walking, is crucial for morbidly obese individuals, as it significantly improves cardiorespiratory fitness and reduces systemic inflammation with the lowest risk of musculoskeletal complications.** Tyrus’s regimen proves that the most effective exercise is the one you can show up for, consistently.
