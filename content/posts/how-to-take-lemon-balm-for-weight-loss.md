### Safety and Interactions: What to Discuss with Your Doctor

Although lemon balm is generally considered safe for most healthy adults, its potent action means it can interact with certain medications. As a specialist, it is our duty to reinforce the importance of safety. **Lemon balm may interfere with thyroid hormone medication, specifically levothyroxine.** This is due to studies suggesting an inhibitory effect on thyroid hormone-binding sites.

**Individuals with pre-existing thyroid conditions must consult a healthcare professional** before initiating a lemon balm regimen, especially if they are medicated. Furthermore, due to its mild sedative properties, it may potentiate the effects of anti-anxiety or sedative medications. We always recommend full transparency with your physician, reinforcing the ethical guidelines of the health and wellness industry.
