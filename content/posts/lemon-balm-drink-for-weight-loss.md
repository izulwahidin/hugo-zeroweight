## Incorporating Trust Signals: How to Ensure Safety and Dosage

### Potential Side Effects and Contraindications (Thyroid, Pregnancy, Medication)

While lemon balm (*Melissa officinalis*) is generally recognized as safe (GRAS) when consumed in food or used in appropriate amounts for tea, it is vital to be aware of potential side effects and interactions. Establishing a foundation of **expertise and reliability** means prioritizing user safety above all else. For instance, individuals with **thyroid conditions** must exercise particular caution. Some in-vitro studies have suggested that compounds in lemon balm may inhibit the binding of Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone (TSH), potentially interfering with the absorption of synthetic thyroid hormones like levothyroxine or impacting natural hormone levels.
