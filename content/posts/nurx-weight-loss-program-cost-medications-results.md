### The Unique Compounded Nurx 3-in-1 Pill

In addition to standard FDA-approved or off-label oral medications, Nurx offers a personalized option known as the "Nurx 3-in-1 Pill." This is a **compounded medication** that combines three active pharmaceutical ingredients for a personalized therapeutic approach. The typical combination includes Bupropion and Naltrexone, alongside a third ingredient like **Metformin** or **Topiramate**.

* **Metformin** is an insulin sensitizer commonly used for Type 2 diabetes but is sometimes used to assist with weight loss by helping control insulin and appetite.

It is absolutely crucial to understand the regulatory status of this compounded pill. According to the FDA, compounded drugs are *not* individually reviewed or evaluated for safety, efficacy, or quality. While compounding pharmacies must follow specific FDA regulations and use active ingredients that *are* FDA-approved, the final compounded product itself is not subject to the same approval process as brand-name drugs like Wegovy.
