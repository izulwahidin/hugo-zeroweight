For many who wish to emulate this progress, safely transitioning from walking to running is a common hurdle. As **Certified Personal Trainer (CPT) Mark Barrows** often advises, "The 'Walk-Run' method is the safest way to build endurance and protect your joints. Start with 60 seconds of jogging followed by 2 minutes of walking, and repeat that cycle for 20 minutes. Gradually increase the running time and decrease the walking time by about 10% each week. This prevents injury and builds genuine cardiorespiratory fitness that sustains long-term results." This professional guidance shows that a structured approach is critical for the long-term success of any new running habit.
