### What is an 86-Hour Water Fast and How Does it Work?

For Dana White, the core strategy to accelerate his dramatic body transformation went beyond simple calorie restriction and involved a powerful, extended 86-hour water fast. ### Cellular Repair and Autophagy: The Advanced Health Benefits

One of the primary goals of an extended fast like the 86-hour protocol is to trigger **autophagy**, a powerful cellular self-cleansing process. The term literally means "self-eating," and it describes how cells break down and recycle old, damaged, or defective components, replacing them with new, healthy ones. In effect, it’s a form of cellular detoxification that proponents suggest can improve overall cellular efficiency, boost metabolism, and may offer neuroprotective benefits. The nutrient deprivation during the fast sends a signal to the body to clear out "cellular junk," and White’s successful results are a compelling testament to the power of pushing the body’s adaptive mechanisms to this advanced level for deep-level rejuvenation.

### Breaking the Fast Safely: The Re-introduction of Nutrients

The end of an extended fast is arguably the most critical and potentially dangerous phase. **This extreme protocol is not for beginners and is a high-risk activity that requires consulting a physician for medical supervision.** Re-introducing food too quickly after a prolonged period of abstinence can trigger refeeding syndrome, a potentially fatal complication caused by severe shifts in fluid and electrolyte levels. To ensure a safe and successful transition, one must break the fast gently and gradually. This typically involves starting with easily digestible liquids like bone broth, which is rich in minerals and gentle on the stomach. Subsequent small, nutrient-dense meals should focus on light proteins, healthy fats, and cooked, low-glycemic vegetables. It is crucial to avoid heavy, sugary, or fried foods for the first 24–48 hours post-fast to prevent overwhelming the digestive system and to maintain the stability of newly regulated metabolic markers. **We strongly recommend that readers do not attempt an extended water fast without professional medical guidance and a thorough pre-fast health assessment.**
