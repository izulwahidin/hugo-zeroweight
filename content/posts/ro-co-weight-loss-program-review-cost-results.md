### Rare but Serious Risks: Pancreatitis, Gallbladder Issues, and Thyroid Concerns

While most side effects are minor, patients must be aware of the rare, but serious, risks associated with the GLP-1 class of drugs.

**Crucially, the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has issued a Black Box Warning** for these medications regarding the risk of thyroid C-cell tumors. This warning is based on studies in rodents, where a direct link was observed, though it is unknown whether these drugs cause thyroid C-cell tumors, including medullary thyroid carcinoma (MTC), in humans. Therefore, individuals with a personal or family history of MTC or those with Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2 (MEN 2) are not candidates for this treatment. Furthermore, patients should be vigilant and report persistent or severe symptoms like **intense, radiating abdominal pain** immediately. This is a crucial step, as it could be a sign of a rare but serious condition like **pancreatitis** or an acute gallbladder issue, requiring immediate medical evaluation.
