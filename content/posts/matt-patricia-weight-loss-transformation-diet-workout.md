DDPY, which stands for **Diamond Dallas Page Yoga**, is a non-traditional fitness program developed by former professional wrestler Diamond Dallas Page. It is a highly effective, low-impact exercise system that utilizes dynamic resistance, which involves engaging your muscles to create tension without the need for traditional weights or high-impact jumping.

Matt Patricia chose DDPY because it provided an ideal solution for a person with significant weight to lose who needed a vigorous workout **without causing further damage to his joints**. DDPY is designed to:

* **Offer a Full-Body Workout:** It blends traditional yoga principles with calisthenics, sports rehabilitation therapy, and dynamic resistance.
* **Be Accessible to All Fitness Levels:** It can be adapted for individuals who are extremely deconditioned or morbidly obese, as well as for elite athletes.
* **Provide Intense Cardio with Low Impact:** The program allows users to get a significant cardiovascular workout, strengthen core muscles, and increase flexibility with almost no stress on the joints, which was crucial for an individual previously carrying a peak weight of 385 pounds.
