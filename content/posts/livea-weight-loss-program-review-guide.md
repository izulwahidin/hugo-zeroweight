## The 3-Phase Journey: From Active Loss to Long-Term Lifestlye

The Active Weight Loss phase is where the initial, rapid results of the Livea program are achieved. The core objective of this stage is to create a significant, yet safe, caloric deficit to promote fast, measurable fat loss while preserving lean muscle mass. This is accomplished by simplifying nutrition and removing the constant "guesswork" and decision fatigue often associated with traditional dieting.

In this initial phase, clients focus almost exclusively on the structured, high-protein, low-glycemic Livea meal replacements. This highly simplified plan allows participants to immediately focus on developing behavioral changes and adhering to a fixed schedule rather than managing complex recipes or food tracking. As clients approach their goal weight, the Livea program transitions into Phase 2, known as the Balance Transition. This critical phase is where the participant begins to shift away from the full structure of the meal replacements and strategically reintroduces grocery-bought, whole foods back into their daily diet. The goal here is to safely and gradually teach the client how to maintain their lost weight using standard food sources.

To provide clear evidence of our methodical approach, we can examine the specific shifts in macronutrient composition and total daily caloric intake between the Active Loss and Balance phases:

In addition to prescription medications, Livea offers certain clients a non-stimulant supplemental approach in the form of Lipotropic (MIC) and B12 injections. These are offered to help support the body’s metabolic processes during the intensive active weight loss phase.

The **Vitamin B12 component** is a vital nutrient that is essential for neurological function and the production of red blood cells.
