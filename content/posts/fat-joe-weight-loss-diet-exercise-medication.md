## Decoding the Diet: Fat Joe's Low-Carb, Portion-Controlled Strategy

Instead, Fat Joe's strategy of disciplined portion control and smart carb reduction provides a flexible, enduring framework that supports a permanent lifestyle change, moving beyond the restrictive mindset of a temporary diet. ### The Combination: Why Cardio and Weightlifting Were Essential

While daily cardio was the engine for immediate calorie expenditure, his comprehensive fitness regimen includes a mix of walking/jogging and **weightlifting**. Cardiovascular exercise, such as walking or jogging, is excellent for improving heart health and creating a significant daily calorie deficit. By incorporating weightlifting, Fat Joe actively worked to maintain a higher resting metabolic rate, which is a key component for **long-term weight management** and preventing the rebound that is so common after extreme dieting.
