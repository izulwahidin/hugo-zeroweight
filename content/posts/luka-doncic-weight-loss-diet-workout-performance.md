### How to Start Intermittent Fasting Safely and Effectively

Doncic utilized the 16/8 Intermittent Fasting (IF) protocol‚Äîfasting for 16 hours and eating during an 8-hour window. When you do break your fast, the quality of that first meal is crucial. Ensure your first meal is loaded with **lean protein**‚Äîa minimum of 20-40 grams is a widely-accepted target‚Äîfor satiety, energy, and muscle support.
