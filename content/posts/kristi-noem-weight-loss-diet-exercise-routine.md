1.  **Prep Your Protein Power Source:** On a Sunday, cook bulk quantities of high-quality protein (e.g., roasted chicken breasts, hard-boiled eggs, or cooked lentils/beans).
2.  **Chop and Store Your Produce:** Wash and chop a large amount of raw vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cucumbers) for grab-and-go snacks. Wash all salad greens.
