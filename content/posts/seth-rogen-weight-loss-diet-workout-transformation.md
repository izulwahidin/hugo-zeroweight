Rogen’s shift to a plant-based, whole-food diet was a game-changer, and you can mimic its core benefits without committing to an overnight dietary overhaul. The goal of this two-week detox is to build new habits and minimize the metabolic shock of sudden restriction. To start, you should systematically replace **one processed meal per day** with a whole-food, plant-based alternative. The physical transformation Rogen achieved hinged on incorporating regular strength training, which helped him not only build a leaner physique but also **prevent potential skin sagging** that often accompanies significant weight loss.
