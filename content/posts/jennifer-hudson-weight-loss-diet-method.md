### Deep Dive into Weight Watchers (WW) and the 'Points' System

The core mechanism of Hudson's success was the WW "Points" system. This system assigns a value to foods based on their nutritional profile (calories, saturated fat, sugar, and protein), effectively guiding the user toward nutrient-dense options. For Jennifer, this meant her strategy was built on **moderation**. Instead of rigid rules, she used the points system to track her intake and ensure she maintained a slight energy deficit, all while having the flexibility to eat her favorite foods. She learned to spend her points budget wisely, prioritizing meals that were satisfying and filling, which is a key component of sustainable weight management according to registered dietitians who advocate for balanced nutrition. The program instilled a level of **accountability** that turned her everyday eating into a measured, successful process.

### The Art of Mindful Eating: Understanding Satiety Cues
