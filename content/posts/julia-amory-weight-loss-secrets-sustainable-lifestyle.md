This ensures that healthy choices are automated rather than constantly relying on willpower. Our proprietary **5-R Rule for Habit Change** is a powerful method for solidifying long-term shifts:

1.  **Reflect:** Honestly assess the habit you wish to change. What triggers the old behavior?
2.  **Replace:** Swap the old, undesirable habit with the new, desired one (e.g., replacing nightly scrolling with reading a book).
3.  **Route:** Link the new habit to an existing one to create a dependable cue (e.g., "After I brush my teeth, I will immediately fill my water bottle").
