Physical change is often a byproduct of emotional and psychological healing, a truth that Lovato’s journey powerfully underscores. The singer's physical recovery is rooted in their sustained work on mental health, including therapy, sobriety, and self-acceptance.

This fundamental shift from aesthetic obsession to functional respect is the bedrock of Lovato's public narrative and a far more trustworthy path to sustainable health.
