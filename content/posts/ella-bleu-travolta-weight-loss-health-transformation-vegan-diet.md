2.  **Phase 2: The '3-Day Block' Commitment.** After successfully mastering Phase 1, commit to three full, consecutive days per week of 100% plant-based eating. 3.  **Phase 3: The '90/10' Lifestyle Lock-In.** Once you are comfortable with the weekly rhythm, aim for a 90% adherence rate to plant-based eating. This allows for flexibility and grace (the remaining 10%) on social occasions or travel, safeguarding the plan against the perfectionism that often leads to failure. This focus on long-term sustainability is the hallmark of a successful lifestyle change.
