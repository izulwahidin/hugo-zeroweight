## The Foundational Science: How Juice Cleanses Drive Initial Weight Loss

In the first few days, this leads to rapid initial weight loss, which is predominantly comprised of water weight and stored glycogen. This phenomenon is consistent with the findings of peer-reviewed research; for instance, a study published in *The American Journal of Clinical Nutrition* examining the short-term metabolic effects of very-low-calorie diets confirms that this immediate and substantial reduction in intake effectively jump-starts the weight loss process by depleting liver and muscle glycogen stores, which are bound to significant amounts of water.

Beyond simple caloric restriction, an optimized juice cleanse contributes to a leaner appearance and lower scale weight by actively reducing systemic inflammation and shedding excess fluid. Vegetable-forward juices are naturally rich in water and essential electrolytes, particularly **potassium**. This high potassium-to-sodium ratio is key, as potassium acts as a natural diuretic, helping to balance fluid levels in the body and counter the effects of high-sodium modern diets that often lead to water retention and a bloated feeling. The flood of easily digestible micronutrients also gives the body's digestive system a "rest," which further reduces inflammation in the gut. By aiding the kidneys in flushing out excess fluids, the cleanse allows the body to quickly shed water weight, contributing to that immediate feeling of lightness and visible reduction in puffiness.
