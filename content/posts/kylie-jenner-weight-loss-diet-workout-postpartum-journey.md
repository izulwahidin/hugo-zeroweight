### The Key Method: Why High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is Her Focus

At the center of Jenner’s routine is **High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)**. This method alternates short bursts of intense anaerobic exercise with less intense recovery periods, making it highly effective for maximizing calorie expenditure in a limited amount of time. For someone with a demanding schedule, this efficiency is key.

Integrating HIIT with **strength training**—specifically compound movements like lunges, squats, and upper body work such as bicep curls—allows Jenner to hit two goals simultaneously: boosting her metabolism through the cardiovascular challenge of HIIT, and building the lean muscle necessary to maintain a defined and toned shape. Studies published in the *Journal of Strength and Conditioning Research* often validate the superior post-exercise metabolic boost achieved through this kind of combined training compared to steady-state cardio alone. Her workouts are often an intense hour long, maximizing the time spent and contributing significantly to her physical goals.
