## The 'Functional' Workout: Laz Alonso's Exercise Routine Breakdown

Laz Alonso’s physical transformation was not solely achieved in the kitchen; his regimen shifted dramatically from the traditional mass-building bodybuilding model to a more dynamic, High-Intensity Functional Training (HIFT) protocol. ### From Bodybuilding Mass to High-Intensity Functional Training (HIFT)

The fundamental difference in Alonso's new approach is the focus on **functionality**—building strength that moves rather than just sheer muscle mass. He traded some of the heavy isolation lifts for High-Intensity Functional Training (HIFT), which emphasizes compound movements (like deadlifts and squats), resistance bands, and bodyweight circuits. This type of training is scientifically recognized for its ability to improve cardiovascular health, boost endurance, and enhance overall body coordination, providing a "cut" look rather than a bulky one.

To ensure his training was always sharp and optimized for fat loss, Alonso has famously collaborated with expert celebrity trainers, including **Mike G.** and **Mike T.** These professionals guide his regimen, which often features a "cutting" workout style—a high-intensity, cardio-driven circuit designed to promote fat burn while preserving lean muscle tissue. A core principle of this HIFT strategy is to keep the heart rate elevated to promote a metabolic state that favors fat oxidation, a process that is far more effective for leaning out than heavy weightlifting alone.
