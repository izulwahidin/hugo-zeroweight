While the clinical validity of hypnotherapy for appetite and behavioral control has been established in scientific literature, full coverage by standard health insurance plans is still relatively uncommon. These accounts are designed to cover qualified medical expenses, and in many jurisdictions, clinical hypnotherapy may be eligible. To ensure your sessions qualify, always request an itemized receipt with the hypnotherapist's credentials and a brief description of the service. Proactively confirm with your FSA/HSA administrator *before* booking to guarantee reimbursement.
