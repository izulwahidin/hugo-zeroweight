With the simple sugars and processed foods eliminated, Kandi shifted her focus to a balanced, nutrient-dense diet centered on whole foods. Additionally, to maintain satiety and energy between meals, Kandi relied on **almonds** as her go-to snack. Almonds provide healthy monounsaturated fats and additional protein and fiber, making them an excellent choice for curbing hunger and avoiding the blood sugar spikes that lead to intense cravings.
