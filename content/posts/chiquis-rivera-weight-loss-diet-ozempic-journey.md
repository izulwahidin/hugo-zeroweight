## The 'Chi-Keto' Plan: Chiquis's Approach to Low-Carb Eating for Latinos

### What is 'Chiquis Keto' and How Does it Adapt Traditional Mexican Cuisine?

The cornerstone of Chiquis Rivera’s long-term success is her signature eating plan, which she has dubbed "Chi-Keto." This approach, detailed extensively in her published work, is fundamentally a **realistic, grain-free, low-carb methodology** designed specifically to be sustainable within the context of traditional Mexican flavors. Unlike highly restrictive diets that demand the elimination of entire food groups, Chi-Keto focuses on incorporating quality fats and moderate protein while carefully limiting high-carbohydrate staples, such as rice and tortillas, that are central to many Mexican dishes. The genius of the plan lies in its adaptability: it teaches followers how to modify beloved cultural foods—like using lettuce wraps instead of flour tortillas for tacos, or swapping out high-starch sides for vegetable alternatives—thereby making a low-carb life enjoyable and permanent, not a temporary, punishing fix. This customization is a critical element for adherence.

### Expert Commentary on Semaglutide (Ozempic/Wegovy) in Celebrity Weight Management

Regarding the responsible use of such medications, Dr. Robert Kushner, MD, a board-certified professor of Medicine in the Division of Endocrinology, Metabolism and Molecular Medicine, has highlighted the importance of these drugs in effectively treating obesity as a chronic disease. Kushner notes that for selected individuals, these medications offer a significantly higher chance of achieving clinically meaningful weight loss—up to 15% or 20% of their body weight—compared to older medications. However, medical professionals uniformly stress that this medication is most effective and used responsibly **in conjunction with comprehensive lifestyle changes**, including diet and exercise.

## The Fitness Routine: Exercises That Helped Chiquis Achieve a Size 6

### Interval Training and Cardio: Maximizing Fat Burn in Shorter Sessions

The transformation Chiquis Rivera saw was not the result of countless hours grinding away on a treadmill; rather, it stemmed from a strategic shift to smarter, more efficient movement. She moved away from the mindset of long, intense, steady-state workouts and adopted **interval training** to specifically target fat burning. This approach focuses on alternating short bursts of high-intensity exercise with periods of lower-intensity recovery, which is proven to be superior for metabolic rate elevation and post-exercise oxygen consumption (EPOC)—also known as the "afterburn effect."

A key component of her routine was **incline walking**. By setting the treadmill to a steep grade and maintaining a brisk pace, she engaged the major leg and glute muscles more effectively than on a flat surface, translating to greater calorie expenditure in a shorter time. Her personal trainer, **Sarah Koudouzian**, has publicly validated this method. Koudouzian’s expertise is clear in her endorsement of this strategy, directly contradicting the common "no pain, no gain" myth. According to the trainer, interval training is not only more efficient but also reduces the risk of injury and burnout associated with endlessly long sessions, making it a sustainable choice for achieving a size 6 from a size 12. This focus on verifiable results and expert-backed methods provides a reliable foundation for the fitness component of her journey.
