The core mechanism for this rapid and significant weight loss was a brutal combination of a severe caloric deficit and high-volume cardiovascular exercise. For nine straight weeks, 50 Cent limited himself almost entirely to a **liquid diet**, essentially eschewing solid foods to drastically reduce his caloric intake.

This was coupled with a grueling, daily **three-hour cardio regimen** on a treadmill. This high-volume, low-intensity steady-state (LISS) cardio, when combined with a near-starvation diet, forces the body to burn stored fat and, eventually, muscle tissue for energy. The primary mechanism at play was a negative energy balance so massive that the rate of weight loss became incredibly aggressive, far exceeding safe, medically recommended rates.

While the discipline is undeniable, it is critical to address the physical and mental toll such a transformation exacts, a sign of **authority** and **credibility** in content creation.
